Key Features of Scan and Pay

Scan, Pay, Go! offers contactless shopping that is scalable and cost-effective. The app supports retailers of any size and a wide variety of store formats. It provides smart suggestions at the point of decision and becomes the heart of the customer relationship.

The app offers a super-fast checkout process with an average checkout time of [X] seconds, and no waiting in line. Customers scan and bag items while they shop, then checkout and pay entirely on a smartphone. The app is optimized to provide effortless utility and continuously tested for ease of use with consumers of different demographics.

The app requires a valid email and photo ID for shopping, and the trust algorithm flags some or all purchases for audit depending on the retailer's preference. Sign up is easy; users just enter their email, take a selfie and start shopping. The app knows when the customer is in the store and is ready to go.

Customers can scan items with the app to add them to the shopping cart, where they can see a running total as they shop, including promotions and sale prices. Smart recommendations improve on the traditional circular, making specials more visible, relevant, and timely. Customers can order ahead via the app or schedule a pickup, freeing them to keep shopping.
