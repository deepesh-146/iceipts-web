Warranty/Guarantee

  Management

Warranty Management had been traditionally viewed by companies as a cost of doing business. The costs of warranty management were found to be costing between 4 to 5 percent of the total sales revenue of the company per annum and were considered to be the cost of providing customer satisfaction and as an opportunity to building customer relationship.

Key Features of Warranty/Guarantee Management?

Effective warranty management helps businesses to build trust and loyalty with their customers. Companies that fail to manage their warranties effectively risk losing customers, as well as incurring costly legal liabilities. Overall, ML has the potential to transform warranty and maintenance management, enabling companies to provide better service, reduce costs, and improve customer satisfaction.

The use of ML in warranty management has become increasingly popular due to its ability to automate and improve processes. ML-based tools can help companies to identify warranty issues quickly and take proactive steps to resolve them. ML algorithms can analyze data from sensors and other sources to identify potential issues before they become major problems.

By analyzing warranty data, ML algorithms can help companies identify patterns and trends that can inform product design and development. ML-based tools can also help businesses to provide better customer service by providing personalized support and automating the claims process.

With the help of ML, businesses can reduce the cost and time associated with warranty management, freeing up resources for other critical areas. In addition to warranty management, ML can also be used for predictive maintenance, which helps companies to avoid equipment failure and downtime.
