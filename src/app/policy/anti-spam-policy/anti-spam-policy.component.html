Anti-Spam Policy

iCeipts Technology ("iCeipts", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to maintaining a spam-free environment for our users and subscribers. This Anti-Spam Policy outlines our approach to preventing unsolicited commercial communications, commonly known as spam. By using our services or engaging with our content, you agree to abide by this policy.

1. Definition of Spam

Spam is generally defined as unsolicited or unwanted commercial communications sent via email, text message, or other electronic means. These communications may include, but are not limited to:

a. Promotional offers

b. Marketing materials

c. Advertising content

d. Sales pitches

e. Any other form of commercial messaging sent without prior consent

2. Consent-Based Communication

iCeipts adheres to a strict consent-based approach to communication. We only send commercial messages to individuals who have expressly consented to receive them. This consent may be obtained through explicit opt-in mechanisms, such as subscription forms, checkboxes, or confirmation emails.

3. Compliance with Applicable Laws

We comply with all applicable laws and regulations governing commercial communications, including but not limited to the Information Technology (Amendment) Act, 2008, Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011. Our practices are designed to ensure full compliance with these regulations and to respect the rights and preferences of our users.

4. Unsubscribing from Communications

We provide clear and easy-to-use mechanisms for users to unsubscribe from our communications at any time. Every commercial message sent by iCeipts includes an unsubscribe link or instructions on how to opt out of future communications. Upon receiving an unsubscribe request, we promptly remove the user from our mailing list and cease all further communications.

5. Reporting Spam

If you believe you have received unsolicited commercial communications from iCeipts or have any concerns about our compliance with this Anti-Spam Policy, please contact us immediately at contact@iceipts.com. We take reports of spam seriously and will investigate all complaints promptly.

6. Prohibited Activities

Any use of our services for the purpose of sending spam or engaging in other abusive or deceptive practices is strictly prohibited. This includes, but is not limited to:

a. Sending messages to purchased or rented email lists

b. Using misleading or false information in commercial communications

c. Harvesting email addresses or other contact information without consent

d. Engaging in any form of fraudulent or deceptive behavior

7. Enforcement

iCeipts reserves the right to take appropriate action against individuals or entities found to be in violation of this Anti-Spam Policy. Such actions may include, but are not limited to, termination of service, suspension of accounts, and legal action as warranted by the circumstances.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Anti-Spam Policy or wish to report a violation, please contact us at contact@iceipts.com.
