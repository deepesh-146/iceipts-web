Privacy Policy

iCeipts Technology is committed to protecting the privacy of its Users, Customers, Partners, and Employees (collectively referred to as "Users" or "You") in all interactions, whether at-rest, in-use, or in-motion, to safeguard our business interests and reputation without disrupting day-to-day operations. The following guiding principles govern our approach to privacy:

1. Minimal Data Collection: We only request the minimum amount of information necessary for business transactions or specific purposes, ensuring we gather only essential data.

2. Efficient Data Sharing: We maintain effective, efficient, and sustainable processes for sharing data with employees, partners, and vendors to support business operations.

3. Authorization Mechanisms: Strict approval and authorization mechanisms are in place to control access to collected information, ensuring data integrity and confidentiality.

4. Technology Safeguards: Our tools and applications incorporate strong technologies to provide higher control, visibility, and protection of information usage.

5. Purpose-Specific Collection: All information collected serves a specific purpose, aligning with our commitment to transparency and accountability.

This Privacy Policy elucidates our practices concerning the collection, use, disclosure, transfer, or processing of your data by iCeipts Technology, encompassing various websites, platforms, offline and other services, including but not limited to data delivery and content dissemination through any mobile or internet-connected device (collectively referred to as the "Services").
